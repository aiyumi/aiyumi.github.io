Rockman DASH - "We are the Three Bonne Brothers" Keyboard Arrangements

These are my keyboard arrangements of the song "We are the Three Bonne Brothers", also known as the theme song for the Bonnes, the charismatic air pirates from the Rockman DASH/Mega Man Legends series. Performed and recorded on a Yamaha Motif XF keyboard.

Download/listen:

Piano and flute version (shorter): bonne-brothers01.ogg (OpenDrive) | bonne-brothers01.mp3 (4shared)

Piano and flute version (longer): bonne-brothers02.ogg (OpenDrive) | bonne-brothers02.mp3 (4shared)

Update (2015/04/17): piano and marimba version: marimbonnes01.ogg (OpenDrive) | marimbonnes01.mp3 (4shared)
